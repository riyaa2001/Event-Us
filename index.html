ROUND-1

FASTEST FINGER FIRST- The question will be shown on the screen and 15 seconds will be given for 1 person from each team to privately message the answer.
The teams will be given points according to how quickly they were able to answer.
5 such questions will be asked, in the end the top teams after tallying of the total points will qualify to the next round.
The question asked will have 4 options, and the participants will have to arrange those options in the correct order.

ROUND-2

THE SWAP ROUND- The selected teams will now answer questions pertaining to a particular category or theme.
But the category from which a particular team has to answer questions from will be chosen by the next team. This round will begin with the team scoring the most points in the last round and so on.
Once a category is chosen, 5 questions pertaining to it will be asked. The following Categories will be there-
- TV SHOWS (Friends/TVD/Brooklyn99 etc.)
- ‘Who Am I’ – Clues in the form of statements/audio clips/pictures will be given in order to guess a famous personality or Common Object.
- Complete the Song Lyrics – first 10 sec are played the rest has to be guessed.
- Riddles
- Guess the recipe – from a combination of key ingredients, the recipe has to be guessed.
- Current Affairs
The scoring in this round will be according to the number of options the participant chooses to be given-
For 4 options – 5 points
For 3 options – 10 points
For 2 options – 15 points
If the team fails to answer the question will be passed on for a bonus 10 points.
Few top teams will proceed to the next round.
